Welcome to the RS Slayer Tracker

This is a page where you can keep track of your Slayer tasks. You must at least create an anonymous account to use the functions.

User Account:

Sign in to your account.

Anonymous Account:

Sign in with an anonymous username. All data will not be saved passed current session.

How To

This is a short text instructional on how to use the RS Slayer Tracker.

1. Select your slayer task monster from the drop down, then insert the number of kills on your task, then when you are making your first kill, press "Start Tast"

2. When you finished with your task, click "Stop Task". Insert how much Slayer XP and GP you have gained during the task. Please only include what you have gained from the task.

3. Scroll down to the "Task Log" at the bottom of the page. If you are logged in, these will populate with your latest tasks both this session and last. If you are not logged in, it will only populate from this session.

To see statistics on each slayer task individually, select "Stats" and then click either "My Stats" or "Global Stats". Please note that "My Stats" only works with an account.
